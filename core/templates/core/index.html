What does the RESUME mean ?

A resume is a formal document that summarizes a job applicant's professional qualifications for a position. It typically includes information about their work experience, education, skills, and notable achievements. Resumes are often accompanied by a cover letter, which highlights the most relevant details on the resume and expresses interest in the job or company.

What is Applicant Tracking System-ATS ?

An applicant tracking system - or ATS, for short - is a type of resume scanning software used by recruiters and employers during the hiring process to collect, sort, scan, and rank the job applications they receive for their open positions. The applicant tracking system was initially created for large companies that are accustomed to dealing with several thousand inbound job applications on a weekly basis. Today, approximately 99% of Fortune 500 companies rely on ATS software to help streamline their recruitment process. However, what began as a recruitment solution for large employers has turned into a commonplace tool for companies of all shapes and sizes.
